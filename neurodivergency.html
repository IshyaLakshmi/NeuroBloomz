What is Neurodivergency?

Definition

Neurodivergency refers to the natural variation in how human brains work. It recognizes that some people's brains function differently from what is considered "typical" - and that these differences are not deficits, but simply different ways of thinking, learning, and processing information.

Key Principles

  • Different, not deficient
  • Strengths-based approach
  • Acceptance and inclusion
  • Individual support needs
