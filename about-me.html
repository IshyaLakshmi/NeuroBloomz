My Story

I'm a student in Kochi, India, and I created NeuroBloomz as part of my Personal Project. Through extensive research and collaboration with educational professionals, I've developed this platform to support parents, teachers, and children in their developmental journey.

What Drives Me

My passion for psychology and child development drives my commitment to creating accessible educational resources.
